About Me

My name is Ahmad Naufal Sya'bani Hermawan, commonly called Naufal, and I am a student at SMA Negeri Model Terpadu Bojonegoro. I have hobbies in photography, videography, and graphic design, and I often upload my work to Instagram and YouTube. Due to budget limitations, I create these works using my smartphone and laptop, and sometimes borrow the school's camera. In graphic design, I am still learning by making gfx or recreating other people's designs as part of my practice. In the future, I plan to create my own original designs with various references from outside. Through this portfolio website, I aim to let others get to know me better and showcase some of the work I have created.
